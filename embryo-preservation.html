Embryo preservation



Nowadays, Embryo cryopreservation is an option for any woman who ultimately wants to conceive. We also can say that Embryo preservation is to preserve fertility so it may be possible to have a baby at a later year. It becomes a boon for the women who are carrier oriented and doing late marriage or going under the strict medication for the serious illness like cancer, tumor etc.





Embryo preservation, or embryo freezing, is part of the In Vitro Fertilization (IVF) process. It involves storing one or more embryos at -321 oF temperatures for future use.
According to the best test tube baby center in Noida, freezing multiple eggs increases the doctor's chances of finding healthy eggs later. All embryos are not suitable for freezing so only good quality embryos will be chosen to freeze.

The main purpose of cryopreservation is when there are more embryos than needed for a fresh IVF transfer. The preservation is provide an additional opportunity for pregnancy without undergoing another ovarian stimulation and retrieval through Frozen Embryo Transfer (FET).
The main significant advantage of embryo freezing is that patients can maximize their chance of conceiving with a single IVF cycle. It avoids multiple pregnancies because only one or two embryos are transferred during a cycle.

The woman who use frozen embryos now experiences success rates equal to, or better than, those from fresh embryo transfer cycles.
